Overview

What this product solves.

Target users: Mobile app users, wellness concepts, AI experiments

Many wellness and productivity apps ask users to manually enter their mood. A smarter system can reduce friction by detecting emotional signals and presenting useful mood insights through a simple mobile interface.

The Mood Detector Mobile App is designed as an AI-powered mobile concept where users can scan or input emotional signals, receive mood feedback, and view simple insights. The focus is on a clean user experience and practical AI-assisted interaction.

My Role

From workflow to product structure.
